I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Computer Vision at MBZUAI in Abu Dhabi. My research spans areas like Domain Adaptation, Uncertainty Estimation, Model Calibration, and Vision-Language Models (VLMs). Currently, I am focused on tackling challenges in Model Calibration, enhancing the accuracy of Weather Modeling, and improving Remote Sensing analysis through VLMs.

Advisors: Dr. Salman Khan, Dr. Fahad S Khan.
